   Once the download is finished, locate the downloaded ZIP file on your computer. Right-click the file and select "Extract All" or "Unzip" to extract the contents to a folder.

5. **Upload to Your WordPress Site**

   c. Click on **Add New** and then **Upload Theme**.  
   d. Choose the folder containing the extracted theme files and click **Install Now**.  
   e. Once installed, click on **Activate** to start using the theme.

6. **Customize the Theme**  
   After activation, you can begin customizing your new theme. Locate the **Customize** option under the **Appearance** menu to adjust settings such as colors, fonts, and layout.
